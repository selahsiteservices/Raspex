The Beginning

Where It Started

In 1993, a vision was born — to harness the extraordinary power of the Meeker raspberry and bring it into skincare and nutrition. What started as a passion for natural, science-backed wellness grew into a product line that quietly built one of the most loyal customer bases in natural skincare.

For over three decades, thousands of customers reordered year after year — not because of advertising, but because the products worked. The raspberry seed's rich concentration of antioxidants, ellagic acid, and omega fatty acids delivered results that synthetic formulas simply couldn't match.

"The best skincare I've ever used. I've been reordering for over 12 years and I will never stop." — longtime Raspex customer

Today

The Reimagining

Raspex is back — and elevated. The same commitment to the Meeker raspberry and the science behind it, now paired with modern luxury formulations, premium packaging, and a direct-to-you experience that puts quality first.

We've taken what our grandfather built — a product people loved enough to keep ordering for 30 years — and made it worthy of the next 30. For the loyal customers who never stopped believing in it, and for a new generation discovering the power of what nature grows.

This is family. This is Raspex.
